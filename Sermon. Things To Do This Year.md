1 Thessalonians 5:14-22

1. Things we should work on this year:
   1. Warn the unruly - v. 14
      1. Those that aren't following God
   2. Comfort the discouraged - v. 14
   3. Strengthen the weak - v. 14
   4. Be patient - v. 14
   5. Don't render evil for evil - v. 15
   6. Do good to yourself and all men - v. 15
   7. Rejoice always - v. 16
   8. Pray Always - v. 17
   9. Give thanks for all things - v. 18
      1. Not for problems, but for God's help through them
   10. Quench not the Spirit - v. 19
       1. By being unwilling to follow the Spirit of God
   11. Despise not preaching - v. 20
   12. Investigate to know if something is correct and good - v. 21
   13. Hold onto that which is good - v. 21
   14. Avoid all appearances of evil - v. 22
