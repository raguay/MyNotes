1. Older Christians are to feed the flock - v. 1-4
	1. Elders refer to pastors and older Christians
	2. They are to feed the flock
		1. Not to force the flock, look down upon, insult.
		2. Not for money!
		3. Not for convince sake or being forced to do it.
	3. They are to be examples first, teach second.
2. New Christians are to follow the lead of the older Christians - v. 5
	1. Submit to those that know more about Christ.
	2. We should be willing to submit among ourselves.
	3. No one is more important than another.
3. Humble ourselves to God - v. 6-7
	1. When we humble ourselves, God lifts us up
	2. We can show humbleness to God by casting all our cares upon Him.
		1. He is the leader
		2. He is looking out for us.
4. Satan is the Enemy - v. 8-9
	1. A roaring lion is one that is old and can't hunt.
		1. He makes a lot of noise to cause the prey to flee towards the younger lions.
	2. Satan has no "teeth" now (controlling death)
5. God will help us - v. 10-11
6. Farewell - v. 12-14
