Jonah 1:1-15

1. God calls Jonah to work
	1. Wants to warn Nineveh of their destruction
        1. Their sins are great
        2. They are about to receive judgement
        3. But God wants to give them the opportunity to repent.
2. Jonah's response to God
	1. Jonah refused
        1. He went the opposite way in a boat
        2. He wanted them to be destroyed
	        1. They had killed many in Israel
	        2. He felt they should not receive forgiveness
3. God's correction
	1. God sends a storm
		1. It's a terrible storm that the sailors feared.
	2. The sailors woke up Jonah who was sleeping
	3. They use the world's method of casting lots (จับฉลากกัน)
	    1. Jonah went along, trying to blend in
	    2. He already knew that he was the problem
	    3. God had the lot fall on Jonah
	4. The sailors turned on Jonah
	    1. They threw him in the sea
		    1. At first they tried to row to land
		    2. But in the end, they threw him
	    2. They assumed he would die
	        1. They didn't understand God wants to use him
	        2. The world thinks God couldn't ever use him again
4. Christians sometimes act just like Jonah
    1. When they don't want to follow God
        1. They join in on the world.
    2. When God disciplines them
        1. The world will just throw you away
