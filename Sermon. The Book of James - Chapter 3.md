1. Don't be many masters - v. 1
	1. We are all servants of Jesus
	2. We aren't the master
	3. When we try to be the master of our lives
		1. We receive a greater condemnation than those who sin out of ignorance.
2. We can tame every animal, but not our tongue - v. 2-12
3. Don't envy or cause strife - v. 13-16
	1. Wisdom that moves you to envy and strife isn't from God
	2. It's the work of the flesh and Satan - stopped
4. True wisdom - v. 17-18
	1. Comes from God alone
	2. Has the characteristics of
		1. pure
		2. peaceable
		3. gentle
		4. easy to produce in our lives
		5. Full of mercy
		6. Good fruits
	3. Doesn't have
		1. partiality
		2. hypocrisy
