# Sermon.The Crucifixion

1. Order of the Week
   1. Sunday
      1. Triumphant entry to Jerusalem
      2. Driving out the money changers
      3. Teaching in the Temple
      4. Leaving Jerusalem for the mount of Olives
   2. Monday
      1. Cursing the fig tree
      2. Teaching in the Temple all day
      3. Leaving Jerusalem for the mount of Olives
   3. Tuesday
      1. The fig tree dead
      2. Teaching in the temple
      3. Last Super with the Apostles
      4. The betrayal and capture
   4. Wednesday
      1. Appearing before the Sanhedrin
      2. Caesar first visit
      3. Appearing to Herod
      4. Back to Caesar
      5. Torture and Crucifixion
      6. Around 5:30 pm to before 6:00 pm
         1. Taking off the cross
         2. Placed in the tomb
   5. Thursday, Friday, and Saturday
      1. Jesus' body is in the tomb
      2. The Thursday and Saturday are Sabbath days
         1. Thursday is the high day of unleavened bread
            1. This is called a High Sabbath, not the ordinary Sabbath
            2. They are the day of rest
         2. Saturday is the normal Sabbath day
      3. Friday they prepared the spices for anointing the body, but could not get it done before the next Sabbath started.
   6. Saturday after 6:00 pm
      1. Jesus rises from the dead
      2. No one knows until around 5:00 am on Sunday
         1. It was still dark when the women went to the tomb.
         2. The angel opens the tomb to show Jesus has risen.
         3. No one had to release Jesus.
2. Good Friday isn't possible
   1. Friday to Sunday can't be three days and nights.
   2. To believe in Good Friday is to say that God lied!
3. Jesus allowed himself to die to save us.
   1. The penalty of sin had to be paid
   2. God is the only one that could do it.
4. The question now is: Do you believe?
   1. If so, you can go to heaven.
   2. If not, you will go to Hell!
   3. It is your choice.
