1. The attitude of a pastor
   - Titus 2:14 “… peculiar people, zealous of good works."
	   1. "Good Works" is what the Lord tells us to do
	   2. It's not what we want to do, but what the Lord wants to do.
   - Jude 1:3 “… ye should earnestly contend for the faith which was once delivered unto the saints."
1. The work of a pastor
   2. Study
      - 2 Timothy 2:15: 'Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.'
   3. Preach
      - 2 Timothy 4:2: 'Preach the word; be instant in season, out of season; reprove, rebuke, exhort with all longsuffering and doctrine.
   4. Win the lost
      - Mark 16:15: 'And he said unto them, Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature.'
   5. Shepard the flock
      - John 21:16-17
		1. Be an example of a good Christian
		2. Give them what the Father gives you to give them
	        1. It shouldn't be the pastor's idea, but God's idea
		3. Weep when they do, laugh when they do
		4. Be available to the members whenever they need you
		5. Keep going even when you don’t feel like it
			1. With a good attitude 
		6. Keep the church in good working order
	        1. Make sure the things that the church needs are purchased
	        2. Make sure everything is in good repair
	        3. fix or get fixed that which is breaking
	        4. Make sure every member knows the needs of the church
		7. Keep records of the church
			1. Salvations - name and date
			2. Baptisms - name and date
			3. Baptism Certificates
		8. Look for ways to expand the church
	        1. New church plants
	        2. Helping missionaries
	        3. New methods of reaching the lost
