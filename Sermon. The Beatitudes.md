Matthew 5:3-12 Beatitudes ผู้ใด้มีความสุข

1. Poor in Spirit (รู้สึกบกพร่องฝ่ายจิตวิญญาณ) v.3.
   1. This is a realization that nothing we do on our own is righteous
   2. Only by walking with Christ are we ever righteous
   3. They will receive in the kingdom of heaven
2. Mourners (โศกเศร้า) v.4
   1. Sorrow of heart for every sin we do.
      1. Since God created us as servants, we sin everytime we decide our lives for ourselves.
   2. Most people just keep going and not feel bad about not walking with Jesus
   3. They will be comforted
3. Meek (มีใจอ่อนโยน) v.5
   1. Meekness comes from the ability to control a situation but deciding not to
      1. Jesus had the ability to destroy the universe instead of dying on the cross
      2. But, He chose that horrible death to pay the price for all of our sins
   2. Inherit the earth
4. Hunger and thirst after righteousness (หิวกระหายความชอบธรรม) v.6
   1. This is a strong overpowering desire to be like Christ.
   2. You would rather be like Christ than to eat or drink.
   3. You will seek after Christ more than after food!
   4. They shall be filled
5. Merciful (มีใจกรุณา) v. 7 - stopped
   1. Starts by forgiving without them asking for forgiveness
   2. Then you are willing to help those that have wronged you
   3. They shall receive mercy
6. Pure in heart (มีใจบริสุทธิ์) v. 8
   1. Saved and living for God
   2. When you do stumble in sin, you instantly ask forgiveness and try your best never to do it again.
   3. They shall see God
7. Peacemakers (ใดสร้างสันติ) v. 9 - stopped
   1. Leading others to God
   2. Helping others to get along with others.
   3. Never acting in anger, jealousy, etc.
      1. It is a live dedicated to live after God
      2. We can't be peaceful if we react in anger, jealousy, etc.
   4. Called the children of God
8. Persecuted for righteousness (ใดต้องถูกข่มเหงเพราะเหตุความชอบธรรม) v.10
   1. This is being looked down on for following Jesus without saying so
   2. You start to walk righteously, people start to hate you - even other Christians.
   3. Recieving the kingdom of heaven!
9. Reviled, Persecuted, and say all manner of evil against you falsely for Jesus' sake. v. 11
   1. Lot's of rewards in heaven as well
