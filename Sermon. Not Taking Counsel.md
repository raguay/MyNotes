Isaiah 30:1 "Woe to the rebellious children, saith the LORD, that take counsel, but not of me; and that cover with a covering, but not of my spirit, that they may add sin to sin:"
อิสยาห์ 30:1 "พระเยโฮวาห์ตรัสว่า “วิบัติแก่ลูกหลานที่ดื้อดึง ผู้กระทำแผนงาน แต่ไม่ใช่ของเรา ผู้ปกคลุมด้วยเครื่องปกปิด แต่ไม่ใช่ตามพระวิญญาณของเรา เขาจะเพิ่มบาปซ้อนบาป"

1. God calls us to seek out good counsel
   - Proverbs 24:6: 'For by wise counsel thou shalt make thy war: and in multitude of counsellors there is safety.'
   - สุภาษิต 24:6 'เพราะว่าโดยการนำที่ฉลาด เจ้าก็จะเข้าสงครามได้ และด้วยมีที่ปรึกษามากๆก็มีความปลอดภัย'
     1. But, it should be wise counsel, not selfish counsel
     2. God expected Israel to seek His guidance
     3. Just like Israel, Christians should seek God and good Christians for counsel
2. But, here Israel refused God's
   1. Councel
      1. They chose the world's councel over God's
      2. They only wanted their way and sought councel that they wanted
         1. It's not counceling, but agreement
   2. Covering
      1. They wanted to cover their sins
         1. Not to stop it, but to keep others from knowing about it
         2. They do it in secret away from others
         3. They did their "works" to cover up their sins
      2. But they refused God's covering for sin by the Holy Spirit
         1. God forgives and covers all sin by
            1. Us accepting we are wrong and God is right
            2. Sincerely wanting to stop sinning
            3. Asking God for His forgiveness
         2. God has the perfect plan, but we have to be in agreement with Him
   3. God calls these actions "rebellious"!
3. Are we rebellious Christians?
   1. Seeking councel from the world
      1. Making excuses based on our culture
      2. Being just like everyone else instead of one set aside for God
   2. Not be covered by God's Spirit
      1. Sincerely asking for forgiveness
      2. Seeking to walk with Jesus daily
