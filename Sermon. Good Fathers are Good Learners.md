1. A good Christian is one that has learned Christ
   1. A good father needs to be a good Christian
   2. By being a good Christian, Fathers learn from God how to do a good job.
      1. We really mess up when we try it our own way!
2. But, how do you learn Christ?
   1. He isn't visible to the eye or about to touch
   2. We learn Christ by experience with Christ
   3. This verse says that all saved Christian have experienced Christ and learned from Christ personally
3. Learned Christ is
   1. Studying His Word
   2. Allowing Jesus to teach us personally
      1. We have to hear him
      2. Then we have to agree to follow what He says
4. We will then
   1. Get rid of our old self
   2. Put on our new self created in Christ
