1. The miracles of Jesus show four things Jesus has power over.
	1. Power over Life and Death - 3
	2. Power over Spirits - 5
	3. Power over Nature - 9
	4. Power over Health - 22
2. Example of Power over Life and Death
	1. Raising of Lazarus - John 11:1-44 
3. Example of Power over Spirits
	1. Jesus Casts Demons into a Herd of Pigs - Matthew 8:28-33
4. Example of Power over Nature
	1. Jesus Calms a Storm on the Sea - Matthew 8:23-27
5. All of the miracles of Jesus show the same thing
	1. Jesus is God!
