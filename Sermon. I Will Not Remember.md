Jeremiah 31:34 "...saith the LORD: for I will forgive their iniquity, and I will remember their sin no more."
เยเรมีย์ 31:34 "...เพราะเราจะให้อภัยความชั่วช้าของเขา และจะไม่จดจำบาปของเขาทั้งหลายอีกต่อไป"

1. God is teaching us about forgiveness
	1. He forgives our sins and iniquities because He took our penalty.
	2. He then repeats over and over (5 times) that He will never remember our sins
		1. As far is the east to the west!
			1. Back when it was written, everyone thought that meant the distance of this world because the thought it was flat and finite.
			2. But, God knows that the world is round and travel east never gets to the west!
2. Remembering
	1. We have a tendency to forget good things, but we almost never forget bad things.
		1. It's a part of our sin nature
		2. We have to overcome it by God’s grace.
	2. True forgiveness is being able to forget the bad others have done to us.
		1. five times God says he will never remember our sins again.
		2. That is a sure promise from God.
		3. We should do the same with others
			1. If we are walking with God, we have forgiven others like God has forgiven us.
			2. Therefore, remembering their wrongs is a sure sign that we haven't forgiven them and we aren't walking with God.
3. Think about others in your life
	1. Have you foreign them?
		1. Are you still remembering their wrongs.
		2. Or, have you forgotten them like God has?
	2. And then work hard at remembering the good things about them.
