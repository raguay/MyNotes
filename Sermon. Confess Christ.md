Matthew 10:32-33 "Whosoever therefore shall confess me before men, him will I confess also before my Father which is in heaven. But whosoever shall deny me before men, him will I also deny before my Father which is in heaven."

มัทธิว 10:32-33 "เหตุดังนั้นทุกคนที่จะรับเราต่อหน้ามนุษย์ เราจะรับผู้นั้นต่อพระพักตร์พระบิดาของเราผู้ทรงสถิตในสวรรค์ด้วย แต่ผู้ใดจะปฏิเสธเราต่อหน้ามนุษย์ เราจะปฏิเสธผู้นั้นต่อพระพักตร์พระบิดาของเราผู้ทรงสถิตในสวรรค์ด้วย"

1. Friends?
    1. A little girl had a friend for many years
        1. They would walk home from school together - Nit and Ping
    2. One day, Nit didn't acknowledge Ping before her new friends
        1. It hurt Ping's feelings
        2. Ping felt like they had never been friends
            1. were they really friends?
            2. or was Nit just using Ping
                - เพื่อนงิน
2. Before men
    1. When the Bible uses the phrase "men" (มนุษย์), it refers to lost, natural man.
        1. Not a true Christian
            1. One that never trusted Jesus and doesn't pretend to know him
            2. One that does Christian things, but just using Christ to get out of hell.
3. Confess Jesus
    1. Is simply identifying yourself with Jesus while going about in this world
        1. It doesn't have to be a complete witness
        2. It's an attitude of looking to Jesus for guidance throughout the day
        3. Though, we should maintain a time to actually work at witnessing
    2. We need to be willing and able to confess Jesus before the lost world
        1. It's easy in the church since it would be expected of you
        2. But, as we go about our day, people aren't expecting you to talk about Jesus
    3. We should be talking about Jesus everyday
        1. What He has done for you
        2. About His love for others
        3. As we have problems, openly seeking His guidance
4. Denying Jesus
    1. Not talking about Jesus outside of church
    2. No matter how many excuses you have, it shouldn't happen
    3. We should be acknowledging Jesus everyday
        1. What He has done for you
        2. About His love for others
        3. As we have problems, openly seeking His
5. Confessing Jesus is part of getting saved
    - Romans 10:9 'That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved.'
    - โรม 10:9 'คือว่าถ้าท่านจะยอมรับด้วยปากของท่านว่า พระเยซูทรงเป็นองค์พระผู้เป็นเจ้า และเชื่อในจิตใจของท่านว่าพระเจ้าได้ทรงชุบพระองค์ให้เป็นขึ้นมาจากความตาย ท่านจะรอด'
        1. If we aren't willing to confess Jesus before the lost, we are lost as well.
