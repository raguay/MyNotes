1. People that Follow God - v. 1-3
    1. They don't follow the counsels of those that don't follow God - v. 1
        1. They will not just get counsel from anyone
        2. They will notice if their lives match their words
    2. They delight in studying God's Word - v. 2
        1. They will faithfully study God's Word everyday
        2. They have daily devotions that include prayer
            1. You can't delight is His Word without interacting with God
    3. They will prosper - v. 3
        1. Just like a tree with plenty of water
            1. It grows and gives fruit in due season
            2. It doesn't mean there isn't any problems
                1. Trees near water still have problems with insects, lightning, storms with great wind, etc.
                2. Those that follow God still have problems, but at least God is with them to help and guide them.
        2. His work will prosper
            1. Not according to man's standard
            2. But it prospers by doing the work God wants done
2. People that Forget God - v. 4-5
    1. Those that don't do the above aren't following after God
    2. They shall be like the chaff blowing in the wind
        1. No good or lasting results with their lives
        2. Many times their lives will feel very chaotic and out of control
3. God knows everyones way - v. 6
    1. Those that follow God as in v. 1-3
        1. God judges by what we do, not what we say
    2. Those that are "ungodly"
        1. "ungodly" simply means those that don't have God in their lives
            1. Christians are ungodly when they refuse to follow God
            2. Lost people are always ungodly because they know not God
                1. You can't follow who you don't know.
        2. Even though they are saved, their end is to perish
            1. All their works will fade away
            2. Nothing they work on will last
            3. Less likely that their children, grand children, etc will follow God
    3. The lost perish in the Lake of Fire
