1. The qualifications of a pastor
   - 1 Timothy 3:1-13
     • blameless
     • the husband of one wife
     • vigilant
     • sober
     • of good behavior
     • given to hospitality
     • apt to teach
     • Not given to wine
     • no striker
     • not greedy of filthy lucre
     • patient
     • not a brawler
     • not covetous
     • One that rule well his own house
     • Not a novice
     • have a good report of them which are without
2. The attitude of a pastor
   - Titus 2:14 “… peculiar people, zealous of good works."
   - Jude 1:3 “… ye should earnestly contend for the faith which was once delivered unto the saints."
3. The work of a pastor
   1. Study
      - 2 Timothy 2:15: 'Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.'
   2. Preach
      - 2 Timothy 4:2: 'Preach the word; be instant in season, out of season; reprove, rebuke, exhort with all longsuffering and doctrine.
   3. Win the lost
      - Mark 16:15: 'And he said unto them, Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature.'
   4. Shepard the flock
      - John 21:16-17
        - Be an example of a good Christian
        - Give them what the Father gives you to give them
          - It shouldn't be the pastor's idea, but God's idea
        - Weep when they do, laugh when they do
        - Be available to the members whenever they need you
        - Keep going even when you don’t feel like it
			- With a good attitude 
        - Keep the church in good working order
          - Make sure the things that the church needs are purchased
          - Make sure everything is in good repair
            - fix or get fixed that which is breaking
          - Make sure every member knows the needs of the church
        - Look for ways to expand the church
          - New church plants
          - Helping missionaries
          - New methods of reaching the lost
