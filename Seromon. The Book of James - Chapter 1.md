1. Salutation - v. 1
2. Joy of Temptations - v. 2-4 
   1. We should be joyful in problems.
   2. Because:
      1. They passed by God's hands already
      2. God has a method or path to escape.
   3. Our faith tells us that we can handle it because God is helping us.
3. Asking for Wisdom - v. 5-8
   1. This should be done for every circumstance.
   2. It is prideful to think that we don't need God's wisdom in a matter.
4. Blessing on low people and curse to rich people - v. 9-11 - stopped
   1. Simply true because when we don't have much, we are more likely to draw close to God.
   2. The rich often are self sufficient and don't call upon God.
5. Enduring temptations - v. 12-15
   1. Remember: God doesn't give problems, temptations, etc.
   2. God allows Satan to bring these things into out lives for us to see if we will follow God or not.
6. God only gives what is good - v. 16-18
7. Wrath - v. 19-20
8. Folloowing Jesus - v. 21-27
