John 14:1-11

1. Jesus is declaring that he is going away.
	1. His father, God, has a big house.
	2. Jesus is going to prepare a place for them.
	3. Jesus will come back to receive us to Him
2. Jesus says that they know the way.
	1. Thomas says how can we know the way?
	2. Jesus said that He is
		1. The way
			1. Not a way, but the only way
			2. There is no other way to heaven than by Jesus.
				1. Religion will not get you there.
				2. Ceremonies will not get you there.
				3. Good works will not get you there.
				4. Family will not get you there.
		2. The truth
			1. There is no other truth.
			2. God's Word is the only book with 100% truth in it.
		3. And the Life
			1. Jesus gave us life
				- John 1:4 "In him was life; and the life was the light of men."
				- ยอห์น 1:4 ในพระองค์มีชีวิต และชีวิตนั้นเป็นความสว่างของมนุษย์ทั้งปวง"
			2. Jesus died to take away the punishment for our sins
				- 2 Corinthians 5:21 "For he hath made him to be sin for us, who knew no sin; that we might be made the righteousness of God in him."
				- 2 โครินธ์ 5:21 "เพราะว่าพระเจ้าได้ทรงกระทำพระองค์ผู้ทรงไม่มีบาป ให้เป็นความบาปเพราะเห็นแก่เรา เพื่อเราจะได้เป็นคนชอบธรรมของพระเจ้าทางพระองค์"
			3. Jesus gives eternal life to all who will believe in Him.
				- John 1:12 "But as many as received him, to them gave he power to become the sons of God, even to them that believe on his name:"
				- ยอห์น 1:12 "แต่ส่วนบรรดาผู้ที่ต้อนรับพระองค์ พระองค์ทรงประทานอำนาจให้เป็นบุตรของพระเจ้า คือคนทั้งหลายที่เชื่อในพระนามของพระองค์"
					1. Him alone.
					2. Not a mixture of works and Jesus
	3. You can't get to Heaven where the Father is any other way.
		1. Jesus is the only way because only God could pay the penalty for all of our sins!
3. Jesus declares that He and the Father are one!
	1. If you have seen Jesus, you have seen the Father.
	2. The words Jesus speaks are the Father's words!
		1. The Father dwells in Jesus - ie: The Soul
		2. The works that Jesus has done proves that the Father is in Him and Jesus is in the Father!
4. Therefore, do you believe?
