Hebrews 2:15 "And deliver them who through fear of death were all their lifetime subject to bondage."
ฮีบรู 2:15 "และจะได้ทรงช่วยเขาเหล่านั้นให้พ้นจากการเป็นทาสชั่วชีวิต เพราะเหตุกลัวความตาย"

1. People have a natural fear of death.
	1. Because they haven't seen what happens after death.
	2. Most people only believe what they can see or experience.
	3. But, once you experience death, you can't go back.
	4. Therefore, everyone has a fear of death
		1. Or, at least the pain before death!
2. For the lost
	- Hebrews 9:27 "And as it is appointed unto men once to die, but after this the judgment:"
	- ฮีบรู 9:27 "มีข้อกำหนดสำหรับมนุษย์ไว้แล้วว่าจะต้องตายหนหนึ่ง และหลังจากนั้นก็จะมีการพิพากษาฉันใด"
		1. We only live once in this world
		2. When this body dies, our soul goes to either Hell or Heaven
			1. No one lingers in this world
			2. Spirits and demons in this world are fallen angels
				1. They can change what they look like.
		3. Everyone that doesn't accept Jesus, that
			1. He is the creator God
			2. He died in our place
			3. We should serve Him and Him alone.
		4. They can only go to Hell.
3. For the Saved
	- 2 Corinthians 5:8 "We are confident, I say, and willing rather to be absent from the body, and to be present with the Lord."
	- 2โครินธ์ 5:8 "เรามีความมั่นใจ และเราปรารถนาจะอยู่กับองค์พระผู้เป็นเจ้ามากกว่าอยู่ในร่างกายนี้" 
		1. When a Christian dies, they go to Heaven​.
			- 1 Corinthians 15:50-57
		2. It is a joyous day.
		3. We have a heavenly body that doesn't age, get sick, or even ever die again.
		4. If we really believe this, we should live it.
			1. Don't follow after other religious practices. (Most of the time here)
				1. Talk about a typical Christian funeral in the US.
				2. Talk about things in Thai funerals.
			2. Don't live for self.
			3. Give your life to walking with Jesus daily.
4. Therefore, where will you be?
