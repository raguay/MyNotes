1. The rich person - v. 1-6
	1. The description of a rich person that is stingy
	2. They deceive their hired workers.
	3. The just person feels they can't win and does nothing.
		1. Many die due to their lack of money due to them.
2. Call for Christians to be patient - v. 7-11
	1. Bear all problem patiently
	2. Waiting for the Lord's return
	3. Don't grudge others that seem to not have problems.
	4. Be patient like the prophets of old
3. Don't Swear - v. 12
	1. To swear in the bible is to claim to do something just like something else exists.
	2. All swearing is sin since it is being prideful that you can do it.
4. Calling for Prayer - v. 13-15
	1. You have problems - pray!
	2. Take care of the sick
		1. Anoint those them with oil - type of the Holy Spirit
		2. Pray for them to recover
5. Confess your faults - v. 16
	1. Don't ever put on airs that you don't have faults.
	2. We all have faults and we need to fess up to them. - stopped
6. Elijah as an example of prayer - v. 17-18
	1. He prayed and it didn't rain for 3 1/2 years
	2. He prayed again and it rained.
7. Helping a brother recover from sin - v. 19-20
	1. He is saved
	2. You will cover a multitude of sins
		1. Christ's blood covers all sins
		2. One sin that we recognize has other sins to back it up.
