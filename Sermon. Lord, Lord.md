Luke 6:46 "And why call ye me, Lord, Lord, and do not the things which I say?"

ลูกา 6:46 "เหตุไฉนท่านทั้งหลายจึงเรียกเราว่า 'พระองค์เจ้าข้า พระองค์เจ้าข้า' แต่ไม่กระทำตามที่เราบอกนั้น"

1. Lord
	1. Someone is lord when
		1. They have authority over us
		2. They have the power to make us do what they want us to do.
			1. They are able to give us a consequence for not obeying.
	2. Real lord expects those under authority to obey.
		1. In the world, they have means to make people obey.
			1. Fines
			2. Jail
			3. Loss of job
			4. In the army, harder labor!
		2. They exercise that authority in order for the country or business to run properly.
			1. Everyone just running around doing what they want to do creates chaos.
			2. Authority has to be exercised to make things productive.
			3. Can you imagine in the heat of a war, a soldier does what he wants to do instead of what he is told to do?
				1. They will all die when one soldier acts that way!
	3. Calling someone lord means you are willing to obey them.
		1. You will follow their instructions
		2. You will look to them for guidance on how to do the instruction.
		3. You will be faithful to the Lord.
2. Christians
	1. To be saved, we have to acknowledge that Jesus is our lord
		- Romans 10:9 "That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved."
		- โรม 10:9 "คือว่าถ้าท่านจะรับด้วยปากของท่านว่า พระเยซูทรงเป็นองค์พระผู้เป็นเจ้า และเชื่อในจิตใจของท่านว่าพระเจ้าได้ทรงชุบพระองค์ให้เป็นขึ้นมาจากความตาย ท่านจะรอด"
			1. We don't make him our lord
			2. He is our lord due to the fact that He is our creator.
			3. We have to accept His authority over our lives.
			4. Many verbally acknowledge Jesus, but never intend to follow Jesus
				- Matthew 7:21 "Not every one that saith unto me, Lord, Lord, shall enter into the kingdom of heaven; but he that doeth the will of my Father which is in heaven."
				- มัทธิว 7:21 "ผู้เคร่งศาสนาหลายคนหลงหายไปมิใช่ทุกคนที่ร้องแก่เราว่า 'พระองค์เจ้าข้า พระองค์เจ้าข้า' จะได้เข้าในอาณาจักรแห่งสวรรค์ แต่ผู้ที่ปฏิบัติตามพระทัยพระบิดาของเราผู้ทรงสถิตในสวรรค์จึงจะเข้าได้"
	2. Therefore, we call Jesus "lord"
		1. He has the authority in our lives.
		2. He has all power to make us do His will.
	3. But, Jesus is a loving lord
		1. He doesn't force us to obey
		2. He invites us to obey out of love for Him.
3. So, why do many Christians say it, but don't do it.
	1. Many just want a free ride away from hell.
		1. Not understanding that they are now in worse condition.
		2. Their heart is hardened against truly trusting.
		3. They will hold onto their faithful church attendance and show of being a Christian.
		4. But, their heart is far from Christ.
			- Matthew 15:8 "This people draweth nigh unto me with their mouth, and honoureth me with their lips; but their heart is far from me."
			- มัทธิว 15:8 "ประชาชนนี้เข้ามาใกล้เราด้วยปากของเขา และให้เกียรติเราด้วยริมฝีปากของเขา แต่ใจของเขาห่างไกลจากเรา"
	2. Many want to, but they hit problems and fall away.
		1. Their faith wasn't perfect towards God.
			1. They didn't grow in Christ like they should.
			2. Still saved, but now miserable.
		2. They let their love go cold
			- Matthew 24:12 "And because iniquity shall abound, the love of many shall wax cold."
			- มัทธิว 24:12 "ความรักของคนเป็นอันมากจะเยือกเย็นลง เพราะความชั่วช้าจะแผ่ขยายออกไป"
				1. Wanting self will over Jesus' will.
		3. They need to confess their sin and get right with Jesus now!
			1. They longer they wait, the harder it gets to get right with Jesus.
	3. Few seem to truly walk with Jesus
		- Luke 8:21 "And he answered and said unto them, My mother and my brethren are these which hear the word of God, and do it."
		- ลูกา 8:21 "แต่พระองค์ตรัสตอบเขาว่า "มารดาของเรา และพี่น้องของเราคือคนเหล่านั้นที่ได้ฟังพระวจนะของพระเจ้าและกระทำตาม""
			1. These are the ones that really get things done for Jesus.
				1. Because they do what He says!
			2. They follow Him because they Love Him!
			3. They truly acknowledge Jesus is their Lord!
4. Where are you?
	1. Pretending to follow Jesus.
	2. Fallen away from him.
	3. Faithful to him.
