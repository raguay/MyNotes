Hebrews 2:1-3 "Therefore we ought to give the more earnest heed to the things which we have heard, lest at any time we should let them slip. For if the word spoken by angels was stedfast, and every transgression and disobedience received a just recompense of reward; How shall we escape, if we neglect so great salvation; which at the first began to be spoken by the Lord, and was confirmed unto us by them that heard him;"

ฮีบรู 2:1-3 "จงใส่ใจกับความรอดอันยิ่งใหญ่เหตุฉะนั้นเราควรจะสนใจในข้อความเหล่านั้นที่เราได้ยินได้ฟังให้มากขึ้นอีก เพราะมิฉะนั้นในเวลาหนึ่งเวลาใดเราจะห่างไกลไปจากข้อความเหล่านั้น ด้วยว่าถ้าถ้อยคำซึ่งทูตสวรรค์ได้กล่าวไว้นั้นมั่นคง และการละเมิดกับการไม่เชื่อฟังทุกอย่างได้รับผลตอบสนองตามความยุติธรรมแล้ว ดังนั้นถ้าเราละเลยความรอดอันยิ่งใหญ่แล้ว เราจะรอดพ้นไปอย่างไรได้ ความรอดนั้นได้เริ่มขึ้นโดยการประกาศขององค์พระผู้เป็นเจ้าเอง และบรรดาผู้ที่ได้ยินพระองค์ ก็ได้รับรองแก่เราว่าเป็นความจริง"

1. Neglect (ละเลย)
	1. Means to do nothing about something
		1. I'll think about it later.
		2. Maybe when \_\_\_\_\_\_ is done.
	2. The more we neglect something, the less likely we are to ever do it!
	3. Especially since we don't know when we might die and not get it done.
2. Our salvation (ความรอดอันยิ่งใหญ่แล้ว)
	1. For the lost
		1. Nothing else can save us from our sin.
			1. Not money, status, nationality, etc.
		2. To delay often means not to ever decide
			1. A lady was told she had a curable cancer
				1. She needed to have an operation to remove the cancerous tissue.
				2. Done right now, very easy.
			2. But, she delayed the operation until it wasn't curable.
				1. It had spread to other parts of her body.
			3. She died due to her delaying
		3. God isn't judging us.
			1. We are already lost.
			2. If we do nothing, then we get the judgement that we already deserve.
	2. For the saved
		1. Not practicing what we say
			1. I'll start living for Jesus later.
			2. When I have enough money, I'll serve Him
			3. I'll pray more later
			4. I'll read the Bible more later
		2. These things end up never happening
			1. The devil will make sure of it.
3. "How shall we escape" (เราจะรอดพ้นไปอย่างไรได้)
	1. A question that God can't answer​.
		1. Because there is no other way of escape.
			1. He was made lower than the angels, a man, to suffer death in our place.
			2. Being like us, Jesus was able to take our punishment for sin.
			3. But, being a sinless God, He rose again from the dead to give us eternal life.
		2. Jesus is the only way to escape judgment.
			1. No other religion can help
			2. No amount of money can help
			3. No relation ship with others can help.
			4. No technology or medicine can help.
		3. Following Jesus is better than
			1. Angels
			2. People
	2. Therefore, there is no escaping once we neglect our decision about Jesus.
4. Please, don't put off until tomorrow!
