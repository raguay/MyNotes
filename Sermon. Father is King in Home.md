1 Corinthians 11:3 "But I would have you know, that the head of every man is Christ; and the head of the woman is the man; and the head of Christ is God."

1 โครินธ์ 11:3 "แต่ข้าพเจ้าใคร่ให้ท่านทั้งหลายเข้าใจว่า พระคริสต์ทรงเป็นศีรษะของชายทุกคน และชายเป็นศีรษะของหญิง และพระเจ้าทรงเป็นพระเศียรของพระคริสต์"

1. God created a definite order
   1. The Father
   2. The Mother
   3. All the children
2. As we are all subject to Jesus, everyone in the home should willing obey the Father
   1. It doesn't mean the father can be mean
      1. Too many fathers force others to obey in anger
         1. This is a bully and not a father
      2. Christ doesn't make us live for Him
      3. He invites us and waits on us to lovingly say yes!
   2. We are to be loving as Christ is loving.
      1. As the father leads the family in love
      2. They will respond in love
3. Therefore, let us be the King in our homes as Christ is for us
