1. The miracles of Jesus show four things Jesus has power over.
	1. Power over Life and Death - 3
	2. Power over Spirits - 5
	3. Power over Nature - 9
	4. Power over Health - 22
2. Examples of Power over Health
	1. Jesus Heals a Centurion’s Paralyzed Servant in Capernaum
		- Matthew 8:5-13 
			 1. A Roman Centurion asks Jesus to heal his servant, who is paralyzed.
				 1. A centurion is a solder that is over 100 other soldiers.
			 2. Jesus says he will go to his house
				 2. But, the centurion said just say the word.
				 3. He is an authority and says to one "go" and he goes. Another "come" and he comes. To another "do this" and he does it.
				 4. He knows Jesus is God and has absolute authority.
					 1. Therefore, trusts that Jesus' word is enough.
			 3. Jesus declares that he has more faith than anyone in Israel!
			 4. The servant was healed that same hour.
	2. Jesus heals a man born blind by spitting on dirt to make mud and put it in his eyes.
		- John 9:1-12
			1. There is two way a person is born blind:
				1. No eyes at all
				2. The eyes have no pupils
			2. The disciples ask Jesus who sinned, this man or his parents?
			3. Jesus says neither, but that the work of God can be seen.
				1. It is the general sin in the world that caused his blindness.
				2. But, God is going to use it to show his glory.
			4. Jesus heals the man
				1. Puts mud on where his eyes are.
				2. Tells him to go to a pool at the temple to wash
					1. He had to walk without seeing.
				3. The man then could see.
					1. He had eyes that worked.
						1. Adam was made from mud
						2. His eyes came from mud
					2. The brain has no idea what to do with the information from the new eyes
						1. But, now the brain does know
						2. No doctor today could do that.
					3. The healing was really miraculous that only God could do.
	3. Jesus Heals a Boy With an Unclean Spirit
		- Matthew 17:14-20
			1. A man brings his son to Jesus.
				1. The son has an unclean spirit, but most likely was epileptic (โรคลมชัก).
				2. The disciples couldn't cast out the spirit.
			2. Jesus tells the boy’s father to have faith.
				1. He says he believes, but asks to help his unbelief.
				2. That was a great prayer request.
			3. Jesus heals the boy completely.
				1. Cuts and all.
