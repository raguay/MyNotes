1. Wars and Fightings - v. 1-5
	1. All strife comes from lust
	2. We want things our way
		1. It is the worlds way of doing things
		2. It makes us God's enemy
	3. We could ask God
		1. But, God never gives something that we lust after
		2. We need to only look for those things that help us to serve God. 
2. Drawing closer to God - v. 6-10
	1. By being humble, we can draw closer to God
	2. We aren't humble while trying to do things our way.
		1. We need to follow God and do His will
	3. By submitting to God, we can easily defeat the Devil.
		1. But, we have to be wholly submitted to God's will.
3. Don't speak evil - v. 11-12
	1. Evil speaking looks down on others
	2. Definitely not being humble
	3. It is you being the judge, not God.
4. Don't assume what you can do - v. 13-16
	1. We need to acknowledge that our plans don't always work, but God's does.
	2. We need to do things His way
		1. Not just saying "if God allows", but truly seeking out His will
5. Sin is also knowing to do good and not doing it - v. 17
	1. When we know what we should do, but don’t do it.
		1. It is good to help our neighbor, but we don’t do it.
		2. It is good to pray for our neighbor to be saved, but we don’t do it.
		3. etc.
